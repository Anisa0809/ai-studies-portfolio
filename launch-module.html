Launch Module (Introductory Module)

This introductory module provides an overview of postgraduate study in Computing Department and Artificial Intelligence field at the University of Essex Online. It supports students in developing essential academic, technical and reflective skills required for successful postgraduate study.

Module Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the structure and expectations of postgraduate study
  • Develop basic digital, academic writing and research skills
  • Become familiar with online learning tools and collaboration methods
  • Begin reflective practice and professional skills development

Artefacts

The artefacts produced during this module include introductory academic tasks, online discussions and practical activities designed to familiarise students with learning platforms and tools.

  • Induction activities and platform orientation tasks
  • Initial academic writing and referencing exercises
  • Online discussion contributions and peer interaction

Feedback received focused on academic expectations, clarity of communication and engagement with learning activities.

Reflective Piece

What?

This module introduced the structure of postgraduate study and the expectations of learning in an online academic environment. Activities focused on orientation, academic integrity communication, and reflective practice.

So What?

The most significant learning from this module was understanding the importance of independent learning, time management and academic integrity. Engaging with reflective models highlighted how structured reflection can improve learning outcomes and professional development.

What Next?

The skills developed in this module will be applied throughout the programme, particularly in reflective writing, collaboration, and academic research. These foundations will support future modules and professional practice in AI.

Meeting Notes

During this module, meetings and communications took place through online forums, announcements and tutor feedback. These interactions clarified expectations, assessment requirements, and academic standards.

Professional Skills Matrix and Action Plan

This module contributed to the development of the following skills:

  • Academic writing and referencing
  • Digital literacy and online collaboration
  • Reflective practice
  • Time management and independent learning

Action Plan: Continue to develop reflective writing skills and apply structured reflection models consistently across all modules. Improve academic research skills through further reading and practice.
